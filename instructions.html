Guitar Brightness Listening Test

Instructions

  • This test contains five sets, each featuring four recordings of a specific guitar chord played on different guitars.
  • The chords you will hear are: A, C, D, E, and G.
  • In each set, listen carefully to the four recordings and rate them based on their perceived brightness.
  • Use the slider below each sample to assign a brightness rating from least bright (0) to most bright (100).
  • You can replay any sample as many times as needed.
  • For the most accurate assessment, wear headphones while taking the test..
